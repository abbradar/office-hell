Office Hell — src/assets/audio/chunks/kaedalus/
================================================

The audio files in this directory (*.ogg) are derivative works
("Adaptations" in the language of the license below) of a single
musical track by Kaedalus.

Original work
-------------
  Title:    "Crack the Underground Base" (also known as
            "Crack the Underground Base — Action / Chipmusic / Rock")
  Author:   Kaedalus (kaedalus.com)
  Source:   https://opengameart.org/content/crack-the-underground-base-action-chipmusicrock
  License:  Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported
            (CC BY-SA 3.0)

Adaptation
----------
The files in this directory are excerpts ("chunks") cut from the
original track, re-encoded as Ogg Vorbis, and renamed by numeric
chunk index. No new musical material has been added. The chunks are
played back in sequence by the game's stage script to keep encounter
beats aligned with the music.
